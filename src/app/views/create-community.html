New community

You can create any number of new communities. When you create a community, you become the sole owner of the community.

It is important that you keep a backup of your private key and pass phrase, in case you loose your data or change device.

As the owner of a community, you can invite new members, archieve the community (make it read-only) and delete the community (members can make a decision to save backup before removing).

Running a community comes with some responsibility. You need to be online for new members to activate their invitation. For security reasons, the invites are only stored on the owner's device.

A community with no other users, will never be synchronized online and can never be restored from pass phrase. Make sure you invite someone to join your community.

In the future, it might be possible for a community owner to remove a post done by other users.
